Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

NJ Premium Services Inc. ("we," "our," or "us") operates an internal customer relationship management (CRM) tool used solely to run our own exterior services business (window cleaning, pressure washing, landscaping, and related services) in Oakville, Ontario and surrounding areas.

What this application is

This is a private, internal business tool used only by NJ Premium Services Inc. owners and employees. It is not distributed publicly and is not available for use by other businesses or the general public.

Information we collect

Our CRM stores information necessary to run our business, including customer names, contact details, service addresses, job history, quotes, invoices, and payment records. This information is provided directly by our customers or entered by our staff during normal business operations.

QuickBooks Online integration

Our CRM connects to QuickBooks Online solely to synchronize paid invoices and customer records for our own accounting and bookkeeping purposes. We do not share QuickBooks data with any third party. Access tokens are stored securely and are never exposed to end users or included in any client-side code.

Data storage

Data is stored using Supabase (a secure database hosting provider) and is protected by access controls limiting visibility to authorized NJ Premium Services staff only.

Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share customer data with third parties, except as required to operate our business (e.g., our accounting software, QuickBooks Online) or as required by law.
